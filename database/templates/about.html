About

My fascination with the insect world started at the age of 2, where I was obsessed with ladybugs. I became interested in butterflies around the age of 10 and started my own collection. After attending my first Lepidopterists Society (LepSoc) meeting, where I was introduced to the wonderful world of moths, I was hooked on Lepidoptera. My passion for this insect order has only grown since.

While I hold a B.S. in Entomology from Purdue University, I have decided to keep my love of Lepidoptera as a hobby rather than a career, and instead go into web development. This is how this website was born.

I also have a website dedicated to my hobby of photography, which includes many photos of butterflies and other insects. You can view my photography website here, where you can buy prints.
